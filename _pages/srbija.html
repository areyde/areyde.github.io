Since 2022, I am lucky to live in Belgrade, Serbia. The country and the city are so beautiful that I stopped almost all traveling except for work, having found all the necessary diversity of experience right where I live. It was also here that I discovered a real passion for biking. Exploring the wonderful nature and cultural sites of Belgrade, I set out to visit all monasteries and forests in the city's vicinity. Later, I gradually started exploring places further away!
