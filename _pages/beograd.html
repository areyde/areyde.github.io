Since 2022, I am lucky to live in Belgrade, Serbia. The country and the city are so beautiful that I stopped almost all traveling except for work, having found all the necessary diversity of experience right where I live. It was also here that I discovered a real passion for biking. Exploring the wonderful nature and cultural sites of Belgrade, I set out to visit all monasteries and forests in the city's vicinity. This map overlays all of my recent travels, making it glaringly obvious just how much more I have to see!

  • Red lines show traveling on a bicycle.
  • Indigo lines show traveling on foot.
  • Blue markers indicate towns and villages.
  • Orange markers indicate monasteries.
  • Green markers indicate natural wonders, mostly forests
