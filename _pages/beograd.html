Since 2022, I am lucky to live in Belgrade, Serbia. The country and the city are so beautiful that I stopped almost all traveling except for work, having found all the necessary diversity of experience right where I live. It was also here that I discovered a real passion for biking. Exploring the wonderful nature and cultural sites of Belgrade, I set out to visit all monasteries and as many towns and villages as possible in the city's vicinity available by bike. This map overlays all of my recent travels, making it glaringly obvious just how much more I have to see!

  • Red lines show traveling on a bicycle.
  • Blue lines show traveling on foot.
  • Green markers indicate towns and villages (similar to the Travels page).
  • Orange markers indicate monasteries.
